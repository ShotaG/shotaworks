I like to spend time in the ocean

I am a lifeguard at the beach. As part of our training, we spend time getting to know our work environment, and the conditions of our immediate area.

I also like to make stuff

Whether it's furniture for ourselves, toys for the kiddos, or ocean toys for the big kiddos, I like to spend time designing and making "stuff" out of wood in my shop.

And I also like to tinker...

Sometimes it's a surfboard that I made. Other times it is a new fin design that a friend comes up with. Regardless of what it is, I combine my love for the ocean, and for making by riding what my friends & I make.
